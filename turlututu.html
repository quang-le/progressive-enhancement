Powered by the Triforce!

  • f Super efficient

    • With the powers of Triforce, Hylian heroes are very resistant, strong and agile. Equipped with the Master Sword (optional), they become a nearly unstoppable force, cutting through foes and sending energy beams like it's nothing.

  • f Non-intrusive

    • Hylian heroes are reputed for being silent. They won't hassle you with countles request, or bore you with endless boasting. Raid the dungeon, save the princess, bring back the treasure: all action, zero small-talk

  • f Versatile

    • The Master Sword is not the only piece of the Hylian arsenal. On top of general purpose items like bombs and arrows, the Heroes have acces to a wide range of specific -and often magical- equipment: magic wands, capes, a grappling-hook, Pegasus boots...The possibilities are limitless!

  • f More than a just a fighter

    • Since 2017, Hylian heroes have acquired the capacity to cook a wide variety of meals and elixirs. With just a cooking pot, they can turn any ingredient found in the wild into amouth-watering meal!
